Our Story

Started With a Mower. Built on Standards.

Mark's Landscaping was started by Mark, a 15-year-old entrepreneur from Columbus, Ohio — determined to do the job better than anyone else on the block.

What began with mowing neighborhood lawns is growing into a full-service landscaping business. The goal hasn't changed since day one: show up on time, do the work right, and leave every property looking better than before.

Every job is treated like it matters, because it does. Reach out for a free estimate and see the difference firsthand.
